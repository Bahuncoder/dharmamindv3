THE NINE GRAHAS - PLANETARY FORCES IN VEDIC ASTROLOGY
======================================================================


Sun (Surya) - सूर्य
--------------------------------------------------
Represents: Soul, father, authority, government, ego, vitality
Deity: Surya Deva
Exalted in: Aries 10°
Debilitated in: Libra 10°

Positive: Leadership, confidence, nobility, generosity, vitality
Negative: Ego, arrogance, domination, harsh speech

Bhrigu's Teaching: The Sun reveals your soul's purpose. A strong Sun gives leadership and self-confidence. Honor your father and authorities to strengthen Sun.

Moon (Chandra) - चन्द्र
--------------------------------------------------
Represents: Mind, mother, emotions, public, comfort, nurturing
Deity: Soma
Exalted in: Taurus 3°
Debilitated in: Scorpio 3°

Positive: Emotional intelligence, nurturing, intuitive, adaptable
Negative: Moodiness, over-sensitivity, dependency, fluctuation

Bhrigu's Teaching: The Moon governs your mind and emotions. A strong Moon gives mental peace and emotional stability. Honor your mother to strengthen Moon.

Mars (Mangala) - मंगल
--------------------------------------------------
Represents: Energy, courage, siblings, property, sports, anger
Deity: Kartikeya (Skanda)
Exalted in: Capricorn 28°
Debilitated in: Cancer 28°

Positive: Courage, energy, determination, technical skill
Negative: Anger, aggression, impatience, accidents

Bhrigu's Teaching: Mars gives warrior spirit and courage. Channel this energy through exercise, sports, or service. Control anger through discipline.

Mercury (Budha) - बुध
--------------------------------------------------
Represents: Intelligence, communication, business, learning, logic
Deity: Vishnu
Exalted in: Virgo 15°
Debilitated in: Pisces 15°

Positive: Intelligence, wit, communication skill, business acumen
Negative: Nervousness, over-analysis, restlessness, cunning

Bhrigu's Teaching: Mercury rules the intellect. A strong Mercury gives sharp mind and communication skills. Study scriptures to elevate Mercury.

Jupiter (Guru) - गुरु
--------------------------------------------------
Represents: Wisdom, teacher, children, fortune, dharma, expansion
Deity: Brihaspati
Exalted in: Cancer 5°
Debilitated in: Capricorn 5°

Positive: Wisdom, optimism, generosity, dharmic living
Negative: Over-optimism, indulgence, laziness, preachiness

Bhrigu's Teaching: Jupiter is the great benefic, bringing wisdom and fortune. Respect teachers and elders. Study philosophy to strengthen Jupiter.

Venus (Shukra) - शुक्र
--------------------------------------------------
Represents: Love, beauty, luxury, arts, marriage, pleasure
Deity: Shukracharya
Exalted in: Pisces 27°
Debilitated in: Virgo 27°

Positive: Artistic talent, charm, diplomacy, refined taste
Negative: Vanity, laziness, overindulgence, materialism

Bhrigu's Teaching: Venus brings beauty and harmony. A strong Venus gives artistic talents and happy marriage. Practice moderation in pleasures.

Saturn (Shani) - शनि
--------------------------------------------------
Represents: Karma, discipline, delays, servants, sorrow, longevity
Deity: Yama
Exalted in: Libra 20°
Debilitated in: Aries 20°

Positive: Discipline, patience, hard work, justice, longevity
Negative: Delays, pessimism, fear, restrictions, suffering

Bhrigu's Teaching: Saturn is the great teacher through discipline. Accept delays as lessons. Serve the poor and elderly to appease Saturn.

Rahu (North Node) - राहु
--------------------------------------------------
Represents: Desires, illusion, foreign lands, sudden events, materialism
Deity: Durga
Exalted in: Taurus/Gemini
Debilitated in: Scorpio/Sagittarius

Positive: Ambition, innovation, foreign success, unique perspective
Negative: Obsession, confusion, deception, addictions

Bhrigu's Teaching: Rahu creates intense desires and illusions. It can give sudden rise or confusion. Practice meditation to calm Rahu's restless energy.

Ketu (South Node) - केतु
--------------------------------------------------
Represents: Spirituality, detachment, past lives, liberation, mysticism
Deity: Ganesha
Exalted in: Scorpio/Sagittarius
Debilitated in: Taurus/Gemini

Positive: Spiritual insight, intuition, moksha, detachment
Negative: Confusion, isolation, sudden losses, lack of clarity

Bhrigu's Teaching: Ketu brings spiritual liberation through detachment. It shows past life karma. Embrace spirituality to work with Ketu positively.
